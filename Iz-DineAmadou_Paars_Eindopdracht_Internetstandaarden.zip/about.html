About Us

Welcome to Anti Socials Club! We are a group of passionate individuals who want to shed light on the impact of social media on mental health, relationships, and society at large.

In every episode, we tackle different aspects of social media, from addiction to body image issues, and discuss how to navigate it all in this digital age.

Our goal is to foster meaningful conversations and raise awareness on the need for balance in our online and offline lives.

A media criticism and commentary series, focusing on pop culture, social issues, and all things that are Extremely Online! We explore how social media is not always so great.

Our discussions include the grave impact of social media on children’s mental health. We cover the dangers lurking on various platforms and underscore the importance of shame-free dialogue between parents and their kids. As we navigate a world where digital interaction is unavoidable, we provide invaluable insights and actionable advice for safeguarding our children’s mental health in the digital age.

Hosted by Tijn and Iz-Dine Faahriez.
